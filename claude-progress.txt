## KEY DECISIONS MADE THIS SESSION

1. Product category: "dispute evidence assembly platform" NOT "AI rebuttal letter writer"
   This is the anti-commodity core. Locked in DEC-007.

2. Moat is NOT the AI narrative. The moat is structured intake + category-mapped
   evidence logic + formatted PDF output. The narrative is a feature; the pack is the product.

3. Three magic moments are non-negotiable engineering priorities in v1:
   - Category → instant specific checklist (< 300ms, specific not generic)
   - Answers → partial narrative preview (after question 2, professional language)
   - PDF download → looks like a lawyer produced it (visual quality non-negotiable)

4. No guaranteed win language anywhere. This is both a legal requirement and a
   trust signal. Kill phrases documented in Positioning.md.

5. 72-hour file retention is firm. Minimum data liability. Explicit user disclosure required.
